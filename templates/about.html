About

SchoolERP is a comprehensive school management solution developed to automate and streamline academic and administrative processes.

It provides modules for student information, attendance tracking, examination management, results, timetables, and fee management.

With a simple interface and secure data handling, SchoolERP enhances communication, transparency, and productivity across the institution.
